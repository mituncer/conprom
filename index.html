Roles

Consultant

My role involves providing expert guidance and advice in the design process, collaborating with clients, architects, and other stakeholders to develop innovative and practical design solutions. My responsibilities include analyzing project requirements, conducting research, offering design recommendations, and ensuring compliance with relevant standards and regulations. My expertise and insights help optimize the design process, enhance functionality, and achieve the client's objectives.

Project Manager

My role encompasses overseeing the successful execution of construction projects from initiation to completion. I am responsible for managing various aspects, including budgeting, scheduling, resource allocation, and risk management. My duties involve coordinating with clients, stakeholders, architects, engineers, contractors, and subcontractors to ensure effective collaboration and timely project delivery. I monitor progress, address any issues or challenges that arise, and ensure compliance with quality standards and safety regulations. As the leader of the project team, I provide direction, make critical decisions, and facilitate effective communication to achieve project goals within the defined scope, budget, and timeline.

Ombud

My role is to act as an advocate on behalf of the contractor. I serve as a neutral intermediary and facilitator, ensuring that the project proceeds smoothly and that the interests of the party you represent are protected. Your responsibilities include facilitating communication and resolving disputes between different stakeholders involved in the project. You may serve as a liaison between the contractor, client, subcontractors, and other involved parties, helping to clarify expectations, address concerns, and ensure that everyone is working towards the project's success. Additionally, as an Ombud, I am responsible for monitoring project progress, ensuring adherence to contractual obligations, and providing guidance and advice to your represented party. My role involves promoting fairness, transparency, and effective collaboration throughout the project, ultimately aiming to achieve a successful outcome for all involved parties.
